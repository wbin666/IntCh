Request that this session be Cancelled

If you would like to cancel this session(s), open the dropdown menu for "Reason" and choose the most appropriate item. Click "Request that this Session be Cancelled

Requesting a session to be cancelled will result in this session being removed from your calendar. You can confirm this by doing the following.

[How to confirm]

  1. Click "UserCenter-->My Calendar" on the top menu.
  2. Click the date which you cancel your session(s)
  3. You can confirm that the Booking record is removed (Booking Status is Cancelled)
